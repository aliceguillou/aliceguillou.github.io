Pecan's Heaven Brownies

Indulge in a decadent experience with rich chocolate brownies, featuring the irresistible crunch of caramelized pecans and a luscious caramel swirl that creates a truly heavenly treat.

Ingredients

  • 120 grams (1 stick + 1 tablespoon) unsalted butter
  • 60 grams (1/2 cup) milk chocolate
  • 140 grams (5 oz) dark chocolate (100 + 40)
  • 2 eggs
  • 230 grams (1 cup) granulated sugar (150 + 80)
  • 40 grams (1/3 cup) all purpose flour
  • 22 grams (2 teaspoons) flaky salt
  • 125 grams (1 cup) pecans
  • 20 grams (4 teaspoons) water

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a saucepan, pour the water and 80 grams of sugar and bring to the boil. After a few minutes, when the mixture starts to turn golden, add the pecans. Mix, making sure to coat them well. Then spread the mixture on a silicone sheet (or on baking paper). Let it cool, then cut it into pieces using a knife.
  3. Melt the butter and the dark and milk chocolates in a bain-marie (without the 40 grams that we put aside for the end of the recipe). Mix until smooth.
  4. In a bowl, break the eggs, pour in the remaining 150 grams of sugar and mix. Add the butter/chocolate mixture.
  5. Add the flour and fleur de sel, mix. Finish by adding the caramelized pecans and the remaining 40 grams of dark chocolate, mix.
  6. Pour the batter into the buttered mold covered with baking paper. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
